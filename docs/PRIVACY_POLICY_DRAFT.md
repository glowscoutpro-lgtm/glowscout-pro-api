# GlowScout privacy policy draft

Effective date: To be added before launch.

GlowScout helps customers discover highly reviewed beauty professionals near them. This draft privacy policy should be reviewed by an attorney before App Store submission.

## Information we collect

- Search information, such as zip code, town, or city entered by the user.
- Survey preferences, such as desired services, budget range, availability, travel radius, and service preferences.
- Basic technical information needed to operate and secure the service, such as request timestamps and error logs.

## Information we do not collect in the MVP

- GlowScout does not require account registration in the MVP.
- GlowScout does not process bookings or payments in the MVP.
- GlowScout does not sell personal information.

## How we use information

- To search for relevant beauty professionals near the location entered by the user.
- To filter and rank results based on review quality, service fit, and estimated cost.
- To troubleshoot, secure, and improve the app.

## Third-party services

GlowScout uses Google Places data to find and display local business information. Users who open external websites, phone links, or map links are interacting with third-party services governed by those services' own privacy policies.

## Data retention

MVP search and survey data should be retained only as long as needed to operate and improve the service. If user accounts are added in the future, GlowScout should add account controls and in-app account deletion.
