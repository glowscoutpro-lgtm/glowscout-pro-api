# GlowScout expansion plan

GlowScout starts with nails but is structured as a broader beauty and wellness discovery platform.

## Expansion categories

- Brows: brow shaping, tinting, lamination, microblading.
- Lashes: lash extensions, lifts, tints, fills.
- Hair: cuts, color, blowouts, braids, extensions.
- Skin: facials, chemical peels, waxing, esthetics.
- Massage: therapeutic massage, sports massage, lymphatic drainage.
- Wellness: med spas, body contouring, injectables where legally appropriate.

## Shared quality criteria

Every category should keep the same baseline trust filter:

- 4.5-star rating or greater.
- At least 10 Google reviews.
- Operational business status when available.
- Clear contact path such as phone, website, or map link.

## Category-specific surveys

Each category should have its own service survey while reusing the same result model.

For example, lashes could ask:

- Classic, hybrid, volume, mega volume, lash lift, lash tint, fill.
- Desired appointment timing.
- Budget range.
- Natural vs dramatic look.
- Sensitivity or allergy notes.

## Monetization options

- Customer subscription for premium discovery tools.
- Provider subscription for verified menus and enhanced profiles.
- Lead generation for qualified customer inquiries.
- Booking affiliate model where partner platforms allow it.
- Sponsored placement only if clearly labeled and still meeting the quality threshold.
