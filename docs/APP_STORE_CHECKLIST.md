# GlowScout App Store checklist

## Build requirements

- Build the iOS app with Xcode 26 or later and the iOS 26 SDK or later.
- Use a production HTTPS backend URL, not localhost.
- Keep Google API keys on the backend only.
- Turn on the backend and provide reviewer access before App Review.

## App Store Connect metadata

- App name: GlowScout.
- Subtitle: Find trusted beauty pros near you.
- Primary category: Lifestyle.
- Secondary category: Health & Fitness or Shopping, depending on final positioning.
- Keywords: nails, manicure, pedicure, nail salon, beauty, gel manicure, nail tech.
- Age rating: answer the current App Store Connect age-rating questionnaire honestly.

## Privacy

- Publish a privacy policy URL before submission.
- Disclose location/search data and survey preference data in App Privacy details.
- If analytics are added, disclose tracking and data use accurately.
- If accounts are added later, include in-app account deletion.

## Review notes

- Explain that GlowScout filters Google Places results to businesses with 4.5+ stars and 10+ reviews.
- Provide a demo search location such as "Austin, TX" or "60611".
- Explain that service costs are estimates unless explicitly marked verified.
- Confirm that the app does not process bookings or payments in the MVP.

## Pre-submission QA

- Test on a physical iPhone and iPad.
- Test empty location, no selected service, failed backend, and no matching live results.
- Confirm all external links open correctly.
- Confirm Google review attribution requirements are satisfied if full reviews are displayed.
- Replace demo assets and create production icon and screenshots.
