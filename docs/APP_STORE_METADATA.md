## Description

GlowScout helps you discover trusted nail professionals near you. Start with a quick service survey, choose what you are looking for, and search by zip code, town, or city.

GlowScout filters results for quality by showing pros who meet a simple trust standard: at least 10 Google reviews and a 4.5-star rating or higher. Compare options for services like Russian manicures, gel manicures, structured gel, builder gel, dip powder, acrylic full sets, dry pedicures, spa pedicures, classic manicures, and nail art.

For each matching pro, GlowScout shows rating, review count, location, contact actions, review highlights, and estimated service cost ranges. Cost ranges are estimates until confirmed by the salon or technician.

GlowScout starts with nails and is designed to expand into health, wellness, and beauty categories such as brows, lashes, hair, skincare, massage, and more.
